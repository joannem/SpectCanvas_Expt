Spectrogram Canvas User Study

Purpose of this experiment:

To understand how a new sound synthesis interface, Spectrogram Canvas, could help its users understand and recreate sounds more intuitively.

There are 2 points of study in this experiment:

  • Evaluate how well Spectrogram Canvas is able to help users understand and re-create a desired sound through repeated usage of Spectrogram Canvas.
  • Evaluate the usability of Spectrogram Canvas through user feedback.
